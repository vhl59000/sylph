# Product framing

## Lead with this

> Newsquick is news intelligence for prediction market traders. We map every news event to the markets it moves — direct, indirect, and second/third-order — so traders make decisions on structured data instead of Twitter and gut feel.

## Not this

- ❌ "AI-powered trading bot"
- ❌ "Algorithmic trading platform"
- ❌ "Quant tool for prediction markets"

We sell **intelligence and context**, not signals or execution. Traders make the calls. We make their calls better-informed and faster.

## The Bloomberg-moment narrative

Prediction markets are a new asset class — $22B/mo, 85K markets, $10T TAM. Every prior asset class got a dedicated professional news tool: Bloomberg for equities, Newsquawk for FX. Prediction markets have nothing. We're that tool.

## Own-trading positioning

Own-trading is mentioned only as:
1. Proof of product (we use our own intel and put real money on it)
2. Source of proprietary P&L labels that improve the model
3. Additional revenue stream while the SaaS scales

Never lead with own-trading. Never present Newsquick as a fund or a quant shop. SaaS is the business.
